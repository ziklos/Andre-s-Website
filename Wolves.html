Wolves

What do Wolves eat?

Meat

Wolves are carnivorous creatures that usually feed on larged hoofed animals. They also tend to feed on smaller rodents.

Where do Wolves live?

North America, Europe, Asian and North Africa

Wolves can be found in the remote wilderness

How do Wolves behave?

Wolf Packs

Wolves are territorial and like to stick in packs from 6-8. They are lead by an alpha and sometimes the alpha's offspring would be apart of that pack.
