Where do wolves live?

Wolves can be located in North America, Europe, Asian and North Africa. Their habitats are found in the remote wilderness. Here is a look at some of the population numbers in the United States.

Region Wolf Population

How do wolves behave?

Wolves are territorial and like to stick in packs from 6-8. They are lead by an alpha and sometimes the alpha's offspring would be apart of that pack. Here's a look at the wolf pecking order.
